I (male) am now a research assistant at Tsinghua University, advised by Hao Zhao and Hang Zhao. I have bachelor degree from Department of Electronics Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Previously, I worked with Professors Philipp Krähenbühl, Yuxiong Wang, Chao Ma.

My current research interests generally lie in 3D vision in Robotics and Self-drivings, VLM / LLM agent, Generative Model.

  • Foundation Model (VLM/LLM) for Robotics.
  • Generative World Model.
  • Closed-loop Simulation and Agent Modeling in Self-drivings.
Some of my past research interests/experience:
  • 3D Scene Reconstruction and Generation.
  • 3D Object Detection and Perception in Self-drivings.
